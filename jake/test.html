Welcome

Hi, I'm Matthew Malensek, a graduate student in the Computer Science Department at Colorado State University. I work as a research assistant for Shrideep Pallickara and Sangmi Pallickara, and my research interests are centered around distributed systems and data-intensive computing. Some of the projects I am involved with include:

  • Galileo, a high-throughput distributed storage system
  • Granules, a framework for distributed stream processing
  • Failure recovery and fault tolerance functionality for discrete event simulations

This site serves as a place to host my projects, publications, and other miscellaneous things that need somewhere to live on the web.
